FAQ

When does the invoice get created?

After an order has been paid, we will automatically create invoice for you.

How to automate wire transfer method?

You can use payment pairing feature of Fakturoid to automate wire transfer. Article on my blog describes the setup.

Where can I find the credentials?

You can find credentials in section Já after you sign into the Fakturoid.cz. Watch the screencast showing complete walkthrough.

Do I have to pay for Fakturoid.cz?

Fakturoid.cz free account has a limitation of maximum 5 clients. With such limitation you wouldn’t be able to issue an invoice to more than 5 customers. Of course you can use free Fakturoid.cz account to try it out and see if Fakturoid.cz is right fit for you.
